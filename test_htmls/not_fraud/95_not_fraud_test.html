Planning is EASY! Making it happen is HARD!

You and your team have worked hard during the 4th quarter of last year to flesh out a plan for success in 2012. Now it's time to make it all happen. Most Business Leaders have some type of vision of their organization's future.   To realize that vision, many strategic plans are created each year after a huge effort. Now what? .

Executing your strategic plan in a systematic way can be a daunting task but the rewards can be great. Accoridnt to a newly released study from NIST, the benefit-to-cost ratio of implementing strategic plans through a performance excellence program is 820-to-1 .

If you want to take your company's strategy and goals off the shelf, get your team to make your goals their goals, then this semiar is for you. Now is the time to  execute your strategic  plan for 2012 to take your business to the next level!

Featured Speaker:

Matt Lauth

Matt Lauth is the managing partner with Strategy Execution Advisors and is a certified Six Disciplines business coach.  He directly coaches small and midsized businesses on strategic business issues including executive/leadership coaching, strategic planning, strategy implementation/execution, process and change management, team building and performance measurement.

Before founding Strategy Execution Advisors, Matt held a variety of leadership positions at Six Disciplines.  Matt led the development of the learning platform, book publishing, company re-branding, website, software development, partner network and most recently the consulting division of Six Disciplines.  Strategy Execution Advisors is a spin-off of the consulting division from Six Disciplines.  When Matt left Six Disciplines, he was the longest tenured employee with over 9 years leading development of the Six Disciplines Program.

Prior to joining Six Disciplines, Matt spent six years with Solomon Software where he managed product development for a suite of financial software, and before that, spent nine years as a senior leader of IT for a market-leading healthcare medical equipment manufacturer.

Matt is a life-long resident of Ohio and enjoys the outdoors, golfing, volleyball and running. He lives with his wife and two children in Findlay, Ohio.
