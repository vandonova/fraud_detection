Join us for the 2011 FTA Tax Conference for networking opportunities, high profile speakers, the latest thinking and information within your industry.


If you are involved in tax matters, this is a great chance to find out more about news and information that could be affecting you in the near future, as well as providing a fantastic opportunity to network with other members and businesses.


BREAKING NEWS: We are very pleased to announce that Mike Warburton, senior director at Grant Thornton, will be speaking at the conference this year and will be available for questions all morning. This is a fantastic opportunity to meet and speak to Mike, who was featured on the front page of The Times a few weeks ago and is regarded as one of the leaders of his field.
