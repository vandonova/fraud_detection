As we approach our 30 th anniversary, the staff and board have entered into a visioning process, the result of which will be revealed during the 30th Anniversary Celebration. This Vision will include a renewed mission statement that helps bring focus to our values and work, a detailed program structure that enhances our current efforts, and articulated goals for the next five years that bring the staff, board, and volunteers together for a common objective as we go forth in solidarity with the peoples of Central America and Colombia. The Vision Fund will make all of these dreams possible by providing the financial backing needed to go into the future in new and expanded ways.
