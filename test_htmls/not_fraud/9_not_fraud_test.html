What are some potential warning signs of abuse?

How do you report a case of domestic violence?

What are some cultural considerations for working with domestic violence issues in diverse communities?

How does domestic violence affect children?

Register for this training to get the answers to these questions and to learn how to support families with domestic violence issues.

Who should attend:

  • Family Support management and direct service staff
  • Staff of other programs who work with parents, children, youth and seniors

Participants will:

  • Learn to identify potential warning signs of abuse
  • Understand the obstacles people face when trying to leave an abusive relationship
  • Learn the steps to report a domestic violence case
  • Learn techniques to approach the topic of domestic violence with the families you serve
  • Understand how domestic violence affects children
