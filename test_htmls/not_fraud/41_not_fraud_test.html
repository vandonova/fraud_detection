The Financial Executives International (FEI), Association for Strategic Planning (ASP) Chicago Chapter and the Midwest Chapter of the Family Firm Institute (FFI) extend an invitation to a

Moving from a “Family-First” Business to a “Business-First” Family

Keynote Speaker

Drew S. Mendoza, Managing Principal

The Family Business Consulting Group, Inc. ® .

Drew Mendoza specializes in working with adult sibling and cousin teams of family business owners/managers and larger shareholder groups. A public speaker and columnist on family business matters, he has appeared on PBS, CNN, CNBC the local Chicago television networks and National Public Radio’s Marketplace. In addition to his speaking appearances Mr. Mendoza has been quoted in a variety of print publications nationwide. He is co-author of the book Making Sibling Teams Work: The Next Generation . He has presented both internationally and throughout the United States to universities, trade associations, and professional service organizations. Mr. Mendoza provided expert testimony to the U.S. Senate Finance Committee on the impact of estate and gift taxes on family firms.
