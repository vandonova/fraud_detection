Where

We will be at the Seven Springs Group Campground in Cave Creek, 7 miles north of the town of Cave Creek.  See the park's website for more details.

We will be having a BBQ Lunch and Dessert Buffet after the hike, for those who are interested. You're welcome to bring your favorite dessert to share for the Dessert Buffet, but you're not under obligation to do so.

When

The group campgound will be open from 3:00 PM on Friday through noon on Sunday.  Join us Friday and Saturday nights for worship around the campfire.  You can sign up for the BBQ, or just come Saturday morning for the hike.

Location/Directions

Take I-17 north to Carefree Hwy east to Tom Darlington Dr, about 12 miles.  Turn left and go 2 miles to Cave Creek Rd.  Turn right onto Cave Creek Rd, which becomes Seven Springs Rd.  Go 19 miles and follow the Fellowship Adventures signs to the Seven Springs group campgounds. ( Note: the last 7 miles are on a well-graded dirt road.)

Click here to get detailed directions from Calvary Community Church .

Camping

There is no extra charge for camping, just the $6 per day park entrance fee, or a Tonto National Forest Pass.  Space is available for tents, campers, and trailers up to 16 feet (no hook-ups).  The campground has water and toilets.  Campers will be responsible for their own food, other than the Saturday lunch BBQ and Dessert Buffet (if purchased).  Please bring firewood.

Cost

Lunch:  The BBQ is $6.50 per adult, $5 for kids 6 to 12 years old, and free for those under 6.

Park entrance fee:

Hike only:  No fee

Camping:  Tonto National Forest pass required - $6 per car/day.
Passes are available at a number of stores. Click here for a list.

Carpooling

Parking is very limited in the immediate event staging area, so we encourage you to carpool from the central campus.  You can sign up for carpooling during the registration process.  For details on how carpooling works, see the Carpooling section of the "Things to Know" page.

Carpools will meet at Calvary central campus (west side by the mail boxes) – last carpool leaves at 7:10 AM.
