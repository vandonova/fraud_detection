Date: Friday, September 30, 2011
Time: 6:30pm-10pm, *opening remarks at 7pm
Location: 3rd Floor of Chau Chow City, 83 Essex St Boston, MA 02111

*Featuring Janet Wu of 7NBC and performance by The Genki Spark

Join us for ASPIRE's 10 Year Anniversary Banquet! We're bringing together supporters, friends, and volunteers to celebrate leadership, mentorship, and growth.

There will also be a silent auction: items include a lunch with Janet Wu and gift cards to the famous Flour Bakery!

The banquet will also celebrate ASPIRE's Youth Leadership Program and the annual Asian American Women in Leadership Conference, two of the organization's longest running programs. Volunteers and past members will speak about their experiences in ASPIRE.

Honoring:

Emilie Chang: Outstanding Service Award recipient

Paul Lee: Outstanding Supporter Award recipient

Rita Shah: Outstanding Volunteer Award recipient

Dahvy Tran: Nellie Hsu Ling Leadership Award recipient
