Lulu Consulting Workshop at Sustainable Business Exhibition

Title: Environmental Policies and Action Plans – making good business sense
Presenter: Jane Rayner, Director, Lulu Consulting

Implementing environmental policies and green action plans can help boost your business’ reputation and profits. In this interactive workshop delegates will find out:

• What to cover in their environmental policy and plan
• The benefits of implementing plans
• What to do with them once they’ve been written
• How to get staff engaged in the process

The session will include practical exercises, providing a starting point for delegates to develop their own policy and plan back in their workplace.

This workshop is limited to 12 people on a first-come first-served basis.
