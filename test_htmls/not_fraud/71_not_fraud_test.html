Saturday, January 28: Snow Tubing @ Liberty Mountain Resort

Ready to hit the slopes for a tubing adventure? Join us as Mason heads to Liberty Mountain for some good old fashioned wintertime fun! No experience necessary so dress warmly and get on the bus!


Transportation, 2 Hour Tubing Ticket, and Meal Provided.


Meet at Shenandoah Parking Deck Circle at 9:45am to depart.

Travel time is approximately 2 hours. Arrive at Liberty at 12:00 Noon.

Get tickets & tubes, and eat lunch. Tubing from 1:00-3:00pm.

Depart for campus no later than 4:00pm to arrive back around 6:00pm

SPACE IS LIMITED!

This event is open to Mason students only .


Recommended items to bring : warm gloves, boots (or shoes that can withstand the snow), warm coat, and a change of clothes (in case snow clothes get wet). Lunch is either a personal pizza meal or a hot dog meal, so bring snacks or money for snacks if you need it.
