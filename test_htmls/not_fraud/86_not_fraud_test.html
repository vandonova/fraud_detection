Creation Circle

is your chance to express, connect, play and explore.

For the first workshop in 2012, we will reflect on the learnings from 2011, create a "mission statement" or "purpose", and a vision board going into 2012, and give our new year a name or theme.

Vision boards are used in many ways to help refocus and to manifest the life we deserve.

The idea behind this is that when you surround yourself with images of who you want to become, what you want to have, where you want to live, or where you want to vacation, your life changes to match those images and those desires.

  • Reflect on 2011, your achievements, challenges & learnings.
  • Set an intention for 2012, name your year and create a vision board,
  • Put energy into what you are creating for the year ahead.
  • Creativly transform want into have.
  • Take charge in your life, share this experience with others : connect, play & explore.
