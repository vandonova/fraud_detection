Zoe Newson is a 19-year-old local woman who is one of the brightest powerlifters in the country. She is tipped for success at the London 2012 Paralympics

By attending this Silent Auction, you will be helping to raise funds to support Zoe Newson for the London 2012 Paralympics.

You will have the chance to peruse the many prizes and place sealed bids on those that you like the look of. The highest bidder wins, but you won't know who's in the lead until each auction ends!

There will also be spot-auctions every 20 minutes, so expect a fast-paced and exciting evening!

The event will be attended by the Mayor of Ipswich and many members of the local business community, so as well as being a fun night out, it will also be a great networking opportunity. Or just bring your partner, relax and enjoy the thrills!

Ticket price includes cocktails and canapes
