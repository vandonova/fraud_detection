DISTRIKT is a San Francisco based Burning Man electronic music collective and production of the Deep End.  We are fired up to present our first event in L.A!

We're combining our DISTRIKT day party playa vibe & energy of Cinco de Mayo with our friends at Supperclub to organize a very special evening including an all-you-can-eat Cinco de Mayo Taco Bar, Supperclub's famous performance art, and a killer DJ lineup of DISTRIKT residents and guest DJ's.  Come early, get your Taco on, and twirl them off on the dance floor.  Playa-attire with a "cinco" flare strongly encouraged!

All door proceeds go to support DISTRIKT, a 501(c)3 non profit.
