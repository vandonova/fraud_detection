The Annual Charity Silent Auction and Holiday Party | Tuesday, December 13, 6-9pm

The Annual Charity Silent Auction and Holiday Party provides an opportunity to reconnect with old friends and colleagues, and also make some new acquaintances. It's also a great way to introduce somebody new to the group.

If you've attended before, you know that the CeC Charity Auction is one of the best around. Bid on a variety of items, such as fine wines, art, clothing, jewelry, gift certificates and many unique gifts. A portion of the proceeds will benefit a local charity.  Last year, we donated $2000 to St. Anthony’s Kitchen. This year we are donating to San Francisco Food Bank!
