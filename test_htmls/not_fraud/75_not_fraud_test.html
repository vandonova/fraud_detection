LaFutura is an invitation-only summit for executives and leaders from the innovation consulting, trend research, and futures industry.

Last year in Berlin, over 70 leading futurists and innovation experts from 10 countries took steps towards building an innovation community in Europe. Now in its second year, the event has expanded to New York with the goal of bringing global, bold and creative minds together to shape the future of the industry and create a common language between existing innovation approaches. This is our chance to truly define what the innovation industry is all about.
