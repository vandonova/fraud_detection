Reception: BC Mining Week Kick-Off & Mining Person of the Year Award

Monday, May 9 from 5:30 pm – 8:30 pm
Five Sails Restaurant, Pan Pacific Hotel
Suite 410 – 999 Canada Place, Vancouver, BC

Salute to the Mining Industry!

Join friends and colleagues from all segments of the industry to celebrate BC Mining Week and honour the Mining Person of the Year at the kick off reception!  Listen to live jazz,  nibble on delicious food while taking in a spectacular view of Vancouve Harbour!  To top it off  there will be a raffle draw near the end of the evening.

Mining Person of the Year Award

The Mining Person of the Year Award is an initiative of the Mining Association of British Columbia (MABC) to publicly recognize an outstanding individual who has shown leadership in advancing and promoting the mining industry from, or within, British Columbia during the past year.
