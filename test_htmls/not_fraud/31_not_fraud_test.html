Entrepreneurship has emerged over the last two decades as arguably the most potent economic force the world has ever experienced! " The economic climate is changing. Companies are laying off, downsizing, and moving out of the United States. The question often asked is Do you have a Plan B or is excellence for us even possible?

It seems that we are always jumping through hoops – trying to balance work, family, and community obligations. Transformative times call for new ways of doing business and require a creative course of action for strengthening relationships and building new alliances. As we emerge from a volatile financial market environment, now - more than ever - is the time to convene to examine fresh ideas, build trusted networks, and participate in substantive, in-depth conversations about how to raise funds, generate deals, manage the portfolio, and realize exits. So with this in mind, a group of motivated women in Huntsville Alabama are organizing Tennessee Valley's Women in Business luncheon on June 25, 2011 featuring A'Lelia Bundles, the great great granddaughter of the late Madam C.J. Walker as the keynote speaker and would be honored to have you as one of our guest for this spectacular event.

The mission of the Women in Business luncheon is to bring together an influential and powerful inner circle to facilitate sharing of hard-to-get information; encourage women to widen their network and do business with each other; and increase their access to information that will inspire and empower them so that they can advance professionally, develop personally, and provide leadership in their communities, the nation, and the world.

We are proud to be a part of this Women in Business movement to release the fears about what we can achieve and live out the truest expression of ourselves as human beings as we step on to grand stage of our own lives. We sincerely hope that you will be able to accept our invitation to be a part of the great event.
