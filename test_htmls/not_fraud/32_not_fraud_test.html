Do you know how to differentiate your arts organization in 5 seconds? Learn how to speak about your organization using the techniques of Verbal Branding and apply these messaging techniques to your internal culture for mission on-boarding and to unify all sales and marketing communications. Verbal Branding - a new branding platform that combines marketplace differentiation with a fully defined conversational approach to give individuals and entire companies a superior way to sell.  Presented by Stephen Melanson, a professional speaker, consultant, and Verbal Branding expert, with over twenty-seven years experience in business ownership, management, and sales.
