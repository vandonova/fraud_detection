Breakout session topics:
· Deepening your walk with God (your vertical relationship)
· Finding your mate (Genesis 2 method vs. Baskin Robins approach)
· Premarital preparation for guys
· Premarital preparation for gals
· Young marrieds – aligning your marriage with the Word
· Handling opposite sex relations for singles
· Maintaining h ealthy boundaries in relationships
· Dealing with family and parents as a young adult
· Fantasy relationships: On-line relationships, Pornography (men),
Romance books (women)
