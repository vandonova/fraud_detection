4 ASD Kids GALA IN THE PARK

The Marquee, SALT Central Park
Saturday 15th December, 2012.

The major charity function of the 4 ASD Kids Weekend, the Gala brings together Weekend guests, an impressive stable of celebrities, corporate tables, VIP guests & 4 ASD Kids supporters for an elegant evening of fine food, flowing beverages and premium entertainment.

Set in the seaside SALT Central Park under a purpose built and themed marquee this year's Gala evening will pull in the breezy summer moods and casual sophistication the destination provides.

An event not to be missed.

Theme: "Summer Carnival"

  • Premium live entertainment
  • Sumptuous three-course meal
  • Beer, wine, sparkling, juices & soft drinks
  • Celebrity interviews
  • Charity auctions
